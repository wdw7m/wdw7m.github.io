Computing Things That Expire at UVA

  • You eservices password expires after one (1) calendar year from date of last change:
    http://its.virginia.edu/accounts/changelost.html

  • Your personal security certificate (that you would use to make the UVA-Anywhere connection) expires after one year.
    http://its.virginia.edu/identity/certificate/

  • Microsoft Windows and Office licenses expire every 180 days if not connected to the UVA network.
    http://its.virginia.edu/software/mslicenses/activate.html

  • Your Health System computing account password (if you have one) expires every 60 days
    • If you want to update this password proactively (i.e. it has not yet expired) To change a password that has not expired (Outlook Web) go to https://email.healthsystem.virginia.edu/ click options located in the top right corner, click change password.Â  Enter Old Password, New Password, and Confirm New Password, click save.
    • If this password has expired, you must call the HIT Help Desk at 434-924-5334 and ask for a temporary password. Use the temporary password to complete the steps in the bullet point above.
