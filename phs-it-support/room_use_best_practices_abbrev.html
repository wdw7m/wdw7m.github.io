Highly Recommended Best Practices

  • Schedule the room or resource as far ahead of time as possible.
  • Familiarize yourself with the room and equipment ahead of time.
  • Be sure that you have the correct video adapter to connect your laptop to the A/V system
  • Bring your presentation files on a flash drive or email them to your Web-accessible email account.
  • There is only one room with a Health System-managed computer that can be used for presentations.
  • Be considerate of those using the room after you and leave the room the way you found it.
  • If you are planning to stream video from the World Wide Web, know that often there is a problem with too low volume.
